Currency for your brand.

BrandTokens are custom coins of commerce for sales, promotions, user engagement, and more.

How it works

Create. Our platform makes it easy to create a custom token on the Ethereum blockchain.

Campaign. Generate promotional campains with the tools necessary to orgranize them.

Distribute. Send out your custom tokens via social media, email lists, or QR codes.

Redeem. Users redeem their tokens for goods on your site.

Exchange. Users exchange their tokens for other branded tokens, facilitating brand engagement.

Store. Users hodl your coins, storing value in your brand for later redemtion or trading.
